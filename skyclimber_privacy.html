Privacy Policy for Sky Climber

Personal Information
None of your personal information is collected. When you open the app for the first time, a randomly generated code is created within the app. This code is paired with your in-game name to authenticate changes made to your highscore on the leaderboard. When you obtain a highscore, your highscore, in-game name, and authentication code are sent to a dreamlo leaderboard. The in-game leaderboard displays this information except for the code.

Advertising
All ads in the app are served by the AdMob network. AdMob may collect anonymous data in order to deliver customized ads to this app as well as to other apps and websites. In order to do this, AdMob uses a resettable Advertising ID which is unique to you and is provided by Google Play services. No data collected is linked to your identity. To learn more about AdMob's privacy policies, see Google's privacy policy.

Analytics
This app uses Unity Analytics. No identifiable information is collected, only useage data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
I may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
